Vanilla Cake

  • Time: 30min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Wheat Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1 Cup
Vanilla Flavor 2 Tbs

Step 1: Take the wheat flour and mix with water to make it into a consistent paste.
Step 2: Add the Sugar and again mix well.
Step 3: Finally add the Vanilla Flavor. And mix well.
Step 4: Bake the mixture for 20min. Once done, decorate the cake with dry fruits and chocolate pieces.

Chocolate Shake

  • Time: 15min
  • Serves: 20
Ingredients Quantity
Cocoa Solution 1/4 cup
Sugar 1 Cup
Chilled milk 2 Tbs

Step 1: pour this cocoa solution in a blender or mixer.Add some ice cubes and 2.5 cups milk.
Step 2: Blend till smooth.
Step 3: Pour chocolate milkshake in glasses.Then add a scoop of chocolate ice cream.Serve immediately.

Fruit Custard

  • Time: 20min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 2 1/2 cups
Sugar 1 Cup
Custard Powder 3 Tbs
Fruits 1 each

Step 1: Heat milk and sugar in medium flame.
Step 2: Take 3 Tbs custard powder in a separate bowl and mix well with 1/4 milk.
Step 3: When the mixture is smooth, add the same into the milk.
Step 4: Mix well and allow to cool donw.
Step 5: Rinse the fruits and cut them. Pour the custard into the cut fruits.
